Welcome to Ishaan Restaurant

A warm and inviting French bistro nestled in the heart of San Francisco's Financial District. Inspired by classic French cuisine, our menu is designed to delight both the discerning gourmand and those new to the delights of French cooking.

Location & Hours

We are located on the corner of Main St and First St, near the Embarcadero BART station, and a short walk from the Transamerica Pyramid. Street parking is available nearby, or use the Valet service at the Harbor Court Hotel. We are open for lunch and dinner Monday through Friday, and for dinner only on Saturday. We offer indoor dining, as well as outdoor seating on our heated patio. Reservations are recommended, but not required. For private events, please contact us for details and availability.
